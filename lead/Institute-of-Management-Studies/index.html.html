About Us

IMS Ghaziabad is a 30 years old flagship institute of IMS Society Ghaziabad and one of the oldest B School in North India which is accredited Globally by ASIC, U.K, NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) Accredited with 'A' Grade and is a proud member of AACSB Business Education Alliance. IMS Ghaziabad has signed MoU with MCX (India Largest commodity derivatives exchange), BRICS CCI School of Analysis, CSC Academy, IBM, Northern Illinois University, Fort Hays State University, Fredericton University of Canada and Jamia Hamdard to boost research, students & faculty development. IMS Ghaziabad is amongst Top 10 best B-Schools in North India as per latest MBA and B School Rankings.

Admissions

Eligibility Criteria : Applicants who have a minimum of three year Bachelor's degree with 50% marks or equivalent in any discipline recognized by the UGC/ AICTE are eligible to apply for admission to the PGDM programmes. Aspirants with relevant work experience and fresh graduates are also welcomed to apply. The basic eligibility for PGDM admission is a Bachelor’s degree or equivalent after 10+2 or equivalent. Candidates will be shortlisted as per CAT/MAT/ATMA/XAT/CMAT scores. Shortlisted candidates will be further assessed on the basis of candidate’s performance in the Essay Writing, Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds, conducted by FIIB. Application form for admission to FIIB, New Delhi can be filled in both online as well as offline mode. The application fee of INR 1500 can be submitted via Credit/ Debit card, Netbanking or by a Demand Draft in favor of Fortune Institute of Business, New Delhi. It is desired that aspirants complete all academic requirements before joining the program to enable them to keep pace with the rigor and internal assessment schedule of the program.

Placements

This process is usually conducted from the month of September onwards. Companies send their job postings to the CMC to be advertised within the college campus. The posting should have the following details: Company profile Job profile Qualifications Selection criteria/Processes Location Compensation Details Post sending their requirements, most companies choose to conduct a Pre-Placement Talk (PPT) so that they can interact with the MBA graduates. This is followed by scheduling of participating companies for placements by allotting day and time when they can begin their recruitment process. The entire selection procedure of a company has to be carried out within the time frame allotted to them. Companies may invite CVs of interested candidates and shortlist college students on the basis of their profiles.
