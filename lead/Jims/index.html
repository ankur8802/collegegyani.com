About JIMS

Jagan Institute of Management Studies (JIMS) is a front runner B-School of India imparting professional education at graduate and post- graduate level in the fields of management and information technology. The Institute has been working for the attainment of a mission which is to develop highly skilled and professional human resource for industry and business for the past 25 years.
